A Glimpse into My Soul

My Story

I'm the middle child in a family of all girls, and from a young age, I learned the value of hard work. At just 12, I started working to help support my family. While I had to put my education on hold for a year before college due to financial challenges, I was determined to continue learning. My family comes first, My father is half body stroke that makes it difficult for him to find a blue collar job but my father dedication to provide he decided to help and provide despite of his condition by collecting valuable garbage that can trade to money, and my mother tirelessly running her balut stall has instilled in me a deep sense of resilience and dedication. My sisters and I are all driven students, achieving academic success with flying colors. We may face challenges, but we are a strong and supportive family, always striving to reach our goals together.

About Me

I am Mariel, a freshman student of Quezon City University taking Bachelor of Science in Information Technology BSIT. While I strive to be a creative and resourceful problem-solver, I'm constantly working towards becoming the best version of myself. I envision myself as a skilled developer, creating innovative solutions and contributing to cutting-edge technologies. Beyond my professional achievements, I want to be a pillar of support for my family, providing them with financial security and the opportunity to pursue their own dreams. I aspire to be a role model, demonstrating the power of hard work, dedication, and using my abilities to create a better future for myself and those I love.
